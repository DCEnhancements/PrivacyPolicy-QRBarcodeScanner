# Privacy Policy

**DC Enhancements** built the **QRBarcodeScanner** app as a **free utility tool** for scanning QR codes and barcodes. This SERVICE is provided by **DC Enhancements** and is intended for use as is.

This page informs users about our policies regarding data collection, use, and disclosure.

---

## Information Collection and Use

The **QRBarcodeScanner** app does **not collect**, store, or share any personal data or usage information. We do not use any analytics tools or data-tracking mechanisms.

You can use the app fully offline and without providing any personal information.

---

## Data Transmission

The app operates **entirely on-device**. When you scan a QR code or barcode, the decoded data is displayed locally on your device and is **not transmitted or stored** by us.

We do **not** monitor, store, or share the contents of any scanned data.

---

## Third-Party Services and Data Sharing

The app does **not** integrate any third-party services such as analytics, advertising, or cloud storage providers.  
We do **not** share any information with third parties.

---

## Ads and In-App Purchases

The **QRBarcodeScanner** app is:

- **Completely free**
- Contains **no advertisements**
- Has **no in-app purchases**

---

## Security

We prioritize user privacy and data safety by design. Since all scanning and data processing is performed on your device and no data is collected or stored, there is no risk of information being accessed or misused by us.

---

## Cookies

The app does **not use cookies**, nor does it rely on any services that utilize cookies or similar tracking technologies.

---

## Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed toward children under the age of 13. However, since we do not collect any data, no information is knowingly collected from children.
